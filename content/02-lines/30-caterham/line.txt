Title: Caterham
----
Company: South Eastern Railway

----
Text: This branch, the length of which is 4&#8541; miles, turns off to the left from [Caterham Junction](/stations/caterham-junction), and passes the stations of [Kenley](/stations/kenley) and [Warlingham](/stations/warlingham). The line runs through the valley of Caterham, the country about which is hilly, and beautifully diversified.
