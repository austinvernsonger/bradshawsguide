Text: ## Basingstoke to Salisbury, Yeovil, and Exeter

After leaving Basingstoke the line proceeds through a chalky range, from which various parks may be seen extending on every side--blended with scattered villages Passing the stations of [Oakley](/stations/oakley), [Overton](/stations/overton), and [Whitchurch](/stations/whitchurch), we arrive at the important station of [Andover](/stations/andover).
