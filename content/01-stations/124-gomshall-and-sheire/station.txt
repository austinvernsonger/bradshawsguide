Text: Sheire was the residence of Bray, the antiquarian, who edited Evelyn's Memoirs. In the immediate vicinity is *Abinger Hall* (2 miles), the seat of Lord Abinger. *Netley Place* (1&#189; mile). *Albury Park* (1&#189; mile). Near which is *Newland's Corner*, from which a most extensive prospect maybe obtained.
