Text:

Telegraph station
: Ford, 4 miles.

Money Order Office
: Arundel.

A short distance to the north is *Angmering Park* and *Michel Grove*. The line still proceeding through a fine and highly cultivated country, passes the villages of *Poleing* and *Leominster*, at the latter of which there is a curious old church, and arrives at [Littlehampton](littlehampton).
