Text: The church contains tombs of Abbot John, of Chertsey, and Sir J. Denham, father of the poet. In the vicinity are *Egham Lodge*, seat of J. Dobinson, Esq.: *Kingswood Lodge*, Mrs. Read. Runnymede (&#189; mile), memorable as the Council Meadow, where the English Barons compelled King John to sign the Magna Charta on Trinity Monday, 15th June, 1215. *Runnymede House*, seat of N. Reid, Esq.; *Cooper's Hill*, so beautifully described by the poet Denham. We then pass along two miles of beautiful scenery and reach [Virginia Water](virginia-water).
