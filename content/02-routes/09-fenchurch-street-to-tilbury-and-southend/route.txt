Title: Fenchurch Street to Tilbury and Southend
----
Company: London, Tilbury, and Southend Railway
----
Destination: Tilbury and Southend
----
Text: This line commences by a junction with the [Blackwall](/companies/london-and-blackwall) at Stepney, and also with the Eastern Counties at Ilford. Passengers may book at either Bishopsgate or Fenchurch Street; but the latter affords greater facilities as to the number of departures. A few minutes only are occupied in passing the stations of [Bromley](bromley), [Plaistow](plaistow), and [East Ham](east-ham), before arriving at the town of [Barking](barking).
